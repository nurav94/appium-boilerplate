Place your Android APK and/or iOS .app file in this folder.

Examples:
  installationFiles/app-debug.apk      ← Android
  installationFiles/YourApp.app        ← iOS

Then update config.properties:
  android.appPath=installationFiles/app-debug.apk
  ios.appPath=installationFiles/YourApp.app

Note: This folder is in .gitignore — APK/IPA files are NOT committed to Git.
Download the latest build from your CI/CD or ask your developer.
